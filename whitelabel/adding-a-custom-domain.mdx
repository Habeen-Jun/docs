## Overview

You can add a custom domain for your agency in ChatDash to make your brand stand out.

## Adding a Custom Domain

1. Login as an agency user in ChatDash.
2. Go to the Agency Settings -> Agency Whitelabel tab at the left-hand side of the dashboard.
3. Click on the `Add Domain` button.
4. Enter the full domain name you want to add and click `ADD DOMAIN` (Example: `yourdomain.com`)
5. Click `Next` to proceed.
6. Add the following record on your DNS Provider to continue and refresh:
    - **Type**: A
    - **Name**: @
    - **Value**: 76.76.21.21
7. Click `Next` to proceed.
8. Click `Launch` to complete the process.

## FAQs

### How many custom domains can I add?

You can add one custom domain per workspace in ChatDash.
You can purchase additional workspaces to add more custom domains.

### Can I add a subdomain?

Yes, you can add a subdomain as a custom domain in ChatDash.

### Can I remove a custom domain?

Yes, you can remove a custom domain from ChatDash at any time.

### Can I add a custom domain to a client project?

No, you can only add a custom domain to your agency workspace in ChatDash.
